Why Decentralized ?

Fair Pricing Model

Ads pricing is currently under the control of giant Ads companies like Google. With help of a distributed platform like DEAM, we will break the dominance of these companies on the market that will be led to a much more fair pricing model.

Smart Targeting

People can set filters on which categories of Ads they want to watch. Ads publishers (companies) can set their Ads to be shown to which group of people ( filter on age, gender, country, people interests, … )

People can Earn Money

People can watch Ads and Earn money! Yes, we believe that people are spending their time to watch Ads, so they deserve to earn the money not the Ads companies.

DEAM Ads Maker

Make your Ads just with a few clicks!

DEAM platform provides the Ads publishers with a sophisticated Ads maker tool that makes the life easy.

As a publisher, just connect your wallet to the app and start creating amazing Ads, leverage using a wide variety of beautiful and elegant built-in templates.

Set your own preferences, pay the fee in DEAM token and publish your Ads on the network immediately.

You can always track your Ads visits and adjust the settings to improve your success rate.

You can also use DEAM Smart Assistant to guide you through the process and raise your chance to be visited more effectively.

DEAM Wallet

Earn money wherever you are!

Install DEAM Wallet on your mobile, tablet or PC and you can view Ads and earn money everywhere.

You will be paid in DEAM token right into your account whenever you view the Ads.

Hold DEAM tokens in your wallet and you will have more chance to get more Ads.

You can configure your preferences in the settings. For example, which categories of Ads you want to receive.

Different Ads have different amount of rewards in DEAM (set by the publishers). DEAM platform sorts the Ads according to their price (rewards) and will push the more expensive ones to the users first.
